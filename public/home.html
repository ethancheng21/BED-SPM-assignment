Frequently Asked Questions

What is Healthier SG?
Healthier SG is a national preventive care strategy to support better long-term health for Singaporeans.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book appointments online via the SeniorsApp under the "Appointments" section or through your healthcare provider.
Is my personal data secure?
Yes, all data is protected and stored securely according to national healthcare data guidelines.
Where can I find mental wellness resources?
Visit the "Mental Well-Being" section in the navigation bar for curated resources and support services.
